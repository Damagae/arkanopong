How to create a level ?


Example :
5 1
2 9 1 1 1


How to :
COLUMNS	LINES
BRICK_TYPE * number of columns * lines


Brick Types :
0 - Empty space
1 - Normal
2 - Bar Up
3 - Bar Down
4 - Bar speed up
5 - Slow Power
6 - Accelerate Power
7 - Ball size up
8 - More  ball
9 - Add Life
10 - Brick Life = 1
11 - Brick Indescrutible
